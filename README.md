# PowerCast
Powercast is a machine learning project focused on forecasting houshold/grid level energy demand forecasting leveraging deep learning models. The project also compares the performance of advanced deep learning methods against traditional forecasting baselines. 

The goal is to analyze historical energy consumption patterns and predict future energy demand accurately using time-series forecasting techniques.

## Dataset
[Hourly energy demand generation and weather](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/nicholasjhana/energy-consumption-generation-prices-and-weather)
Description: 4 years of electrical consumption, generation, pricing, and weather data for Spain.
